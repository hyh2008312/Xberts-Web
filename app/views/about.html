About Xberts

Xberts is a community where hardware innovators release their new products to collect unbiased reviews and valuable feedback from early adopters like you.

Our Mission

We believe innovation and creativity can improve our lives and make our world a better place. At Xberts, we’re building a new place for people to discover and try the latest innovative products. Our goal is to connect incredible innovators with their most passionate early adopters and evangelists from around the world.

Who We Are

Xberts is a Y Combinator-backed company based in Silicon Valley and China, two of the world’s most dynamic centers of entrepreneurship and innovation. Our team is comprised of hustlers, doers and tech enthusiasts who set out on a journey to bring new inventions to our everyday lives.

Our Partners

We're collaborating with top industry partners supporting innovators and hardware startups around the world.
