Process

  • Step 1:
    Apply for this product review campaign online.

  • Step 2:
    Xberts team will contact successful applicants via Email to confirm his/her shipping information. If the successful applicant does not respond to Email before deadline, Xberts will assume this applicant give up his/her candidacy.

  • Step 3:
    After your shipping address is confirmed, product will be directly shipped to you from campaign owner. Xberts will provide each reviewer with a tracking number and further instructions on review and submission.

  • Step 4:
    After receiving the product, selected applicants will be required to try out the sample, and submit their product reviews by {{ review.date_end |amDateFormat:'MMM DD, YYYY'}}. The review report should include multiple photos or a video.
