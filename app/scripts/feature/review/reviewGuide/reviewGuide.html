Xberts Review Guide

1. Timeline & Deadline

Please finalize and submit your review before {{review.dateEnd|amDateFormat:'MMMM Do, YYYY'}}, which is critical to maintaining your credibility as an Xberts reviewer. If you have inevitable factors that prevent you from submitting on time, please contact Xberts team info@xberts.com ASAP to discuss your individual case. Note that late submission without prior consulting with Xberts may result in temporary or permanent account suspension.

2. Technical Support

If you have any technical issues during your product review, please email our technical support team via review@xberts.com to clear it out. In the email, please include your name and a brief description of the problem and your action. Some screen shot or picture would be much helpful to determine what the problem is.

3. Review Content

While you are encouraged to be creative, a helpful review should include the following:
a) Your unboxing experience
b) Your user experience
c) Three product photos
d) Pros and ConsThe
The following would enrich your review content and enhance your chances ofbeing selected next time:
a) Video Clip of unboxing
b) Video Clip of product reviewing

4. Review Submission Instruction

To submit your review on Xberts, please:
a) Go to https://www.xberts.com/ and log into your account;
b) Go to ‘My Profile’, and click ‘Trials’;
c) Find the right trial, and click 'Submit Review' button to edit and submit your review content;
d) We highly encourage you to insert a creative video into your review.

5. Rating Criteria

star  : 
The product is almost perfect; all features are beyond expectations, and there is nothing to complain about.
star  : 
The product is magnificent; all features work as described.
star  : 
The product is decent; it is functional but still needs a lot of improvement.
star  : 
The product works, but has a major defect.
star  : 
The product is not functioning at all.

Note:Most of products are rated between 3.2 – 3.8 stars on Xberts. We want to collect the most honest and unbiased reviews to help other customers make wise purchasing decision, and we do not promote products for companies through reviews.

6. Review Intellectual Property

Please be mindful that the IP of your review is co-owned by yourself, Product Sponsor and Xberts. All parties are entitled to share the content of your review in other outlets with due recognition to other two parties.
