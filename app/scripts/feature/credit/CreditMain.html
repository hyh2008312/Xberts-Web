FAQ
Is Xberts Legitimate?
Xberts is a YC-funded company that aims to create a community where tech geeks can share their favorite products, we also give out free or discounted samples in order to collect authentic feedbacks for both manufacturers and customers, so our users can make wise purchasing choices.
Can I lose my badge?
It is possible to lose your badge after you redeem free gifts. Also, every half year, we will assessif you have earned enough points to maintain your badge, or you will be downgraded.
Is there a daily limit on the points I can win per day?
The maximum points you can get each day is 100 points.
Am I guaranteed to claim free samples?
Usually we give out limited number of free samples. priority will be given to those who have higher rank and more points, however, you can always redeem free gifts, and we will deduct your points accordingly.
Will these gifts be changed?
We will update the free gifts every month, so you can have more options on what to redeem!
What kind of product suggestion/review will be featured?
We do prefer creative contents and self-made videos. As long as you take your time to write a quality product suggestion/review, we'd love to reward you with more bonus points!
