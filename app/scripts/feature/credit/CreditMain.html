FAQ
Is Xberts Legitimate?
Xberts is a YC-funded company that aims to create a community where tech geeks can share their favorite products, we also give out free or discounted samples in order to collect authentic reviews for both manufacturers and customers. That way, the manufacturers can make improvements on their product and our users can make wise purchasing decision.
Is there a daily limit on the points I can win per day?
The maximum points you can get each day is 100 points.
Will these gifts be changed?
We will update the free gifts every month, so you can have more options on what to redeem!
What kind of product sharing/review will be featured?
We do prefer creative contents and self-made videos. As long as you take your time to write a quality product sharing/review, we'd love to reward you with more bonus points!
How can I see how many points I have earned?
You can check your total points balance by logging in to Xberts and clicking on “My Profile” page. Your total points will be displayed at the top of your profile page.
How long can I receive the product after submitting the gift redemption request?
The product will normally be shipped within 5 working days after we receive your redemption request. We will send you a shipping notification and tracking number through email. Please note that the delivery time will depend on the shipping destination.
What kind of contents and behaviors will be treated as spam?
If you flood the community with posts, comments, fake user invitations or illegal contents, you may be considered a spammer. Once you’re treated as a spammer, your account may be blocked or removed all the points from your record.
